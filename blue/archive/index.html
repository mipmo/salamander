By now, you will probably have noticed the very blue nature of this website. I would briefly like to explain this choice. It just so happens, that blue is my favourite colour. The shades used on this website are inspired by one particular image: A picture of clouds taken by Alexandre Debieve, found on Unsplash.

The eagle-eyed among you may have realised that my picture is considerable more blue than the original by Alexandre. I took the liberty of adjusting the colours of the image to fit a very particular Ghibli-esque look that I had in mind.

In future updates, I might use the image more prominently as a design element on this pointless website. We'll see about that. Toodle-oo!
